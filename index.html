ABOUT

I work as a full-time, freelance illustrator and
have been able to travel the world while supporting myself through my art. Over the last two years
I’ve spent time traveling through Iceland in a camper van and cozied up in a studio apartment in Amsterdam.
I’ve bopped all over Mexico and lived more van-life adventures cruising around New Zealand.
I’ve had more oat-milk lattes then I can count and have spent many late nights working from the road,
and have plenty of things to share from these experiences.
A travel blog seemed like an inevitable next step!
